# Thesis Writing Guidelines (Template)

Use these notes as a generic checklist and replace with your own project-specific guidance.

## Abstract
- State the problem, your approach, the main result, and the key takeaway.

## Introduction
- Provide context and motivation.
- Define the research question and objectives.
- Summarize contributions.

## Methods
- Describe data, materials, or experimental setup.
- Explain procedures and analysis steps clearly enough to reproduce.

## Results
- Present findings with figures and tables.
- Highlight the most important outcomes.

## Discussion
- Interpret results, compare with related work, and note limitations.

## Conclusion
- Summarize contributions and suggest future work.
